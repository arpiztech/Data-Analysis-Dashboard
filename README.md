# 📊 Aalam-Store-Data-Analysis (Interactive Dashboard using MS Excel)

## 🧾 Project Objective

The Aalam Store aims to create an annual sales report for 2024 to better understand customer behavior and identify growth opportunities for 2025 & 2026.

---

## 📁 Dataset Used

* Sales dataset containing order details, customer demographics, product categories, and delivery status.
* <a href="https://github.com/arpiztech/Data-Analysis-Dashboard/blob/main/Aalam%20Store%20Report.xlsx">Aalam Store Dataset</a>

---

## ❓ Questions (KPIs)

* Compare the sales and orders using a single chart.
* Which month recorded the highest sales and number of orders?
* Who purchased more – Men or Women?
* What are the different order statuses in 2024?
* List the top 10 states contributing to total sales.
* What is the relationship between age and gender based on number of orders?
* Which sales channel contributes the most to revenue?
* What is the highest selling product category?
* What percentage of total orders were successfully delivered?

---

## ⚙️ Process

* Verified dataset for missing values and anomalies, and cleaned the data accordingly.
* Ensured consistency in data types, formats, and values.
* Created multiple pivot tables based on business questions.
* Designed an interactive dashboard by combining all pivot tables.
* Added slicers and filters to enable dynamic data visualization.

---

## 📊 Dashboard

* Interactive Excel dashboard with slicers for category, state, and channel.
* Visual representation using charts (bar, pie, line) for better insights.
*  <a href="https://github.com/arpiztech/Data-Analysis-Dashboard/blob/main/Aalam%20Report.png">View Dashboard</a>

---

## 🔍 Project Insights

* Women customers contribute a higher share of purchases (~60–65%).
* Top-performing states include Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh.
* The adult age group (30–49 years) contributes the highest number of orders (~50%).
* Major sales channels are Amazon, Flipkart, and Myntra.
* Most orders fall under “Delivered” status, indicating strong logistics performance.
* Clothing and lifestyle categories are among the top-selling segments.

---

## ✅ Final Conclusion

To improve the sales of Aalam Store, a strategic marketing plan focused on women aged 30-49 years residing in Maharashtra, Karnataka, and Uttar Pradesh should be implemented. This demographic represents a key consumer segment, as they often make significant household and lifestyle purchases. The approach should include targeted digital marketing campaigns and personalized promotions to capture their attention.
